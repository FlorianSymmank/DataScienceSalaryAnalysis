Principal Business Systems Analyst (m/f/d) is a key role in the Business Analytics & Reporting organization within MKS IT. Reporting to the Sr. Manager of Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence Reporting, BSA will work with busi-ness and IT partners to gather business intelligence reporting and analytic requirements, write functional specification documents to map data from applications, create report & dashboard wireframes, conduct detailed source data analysis, identify data gaps and work with the business and technical teams to implement high quality BI solutions. This role doesn't have leadership responsibilities.





Your responsibilities

Works on multiple enterprise BI/Data analytics projects involving divi-sional and cross divisional requirements across various corporate business functions
Translate business needs into prioritized set of data, reports, and dash-board requirements; Document business requirements in a formal Business Requirements Document (BRD), obtain business sign off and turn them to Functional Specification Document (FSD) for implementation
Partner with project managers and Data/BI architects to create solutions roadmap and delivery plans
Document test plans and test scripts working with business and IT stakeholders; Lead integration testing and user acceptance testing, track issues and drive them to closure
Act as a functional SME and a business advisor for reporting and data analytics solutions; troubleshoot report/data issues working with business and technical stakeholders
Work with business and application owners to improve process and data quality issues that affect reporting solutions; Conduct data reconciliations against sources and provide feedback to the technical team to ensure data products meet expected data integrity and accuracy
Develop BI training content and deliver regular trainings to end users to drive adoption of BI solutions



What you bring to the table

Bachelor or master’s degree in business, information science or a related field with an appropriate IT experience (consulting or full time)
Appropriate experience for this role in the field of implementing Business Intelligence solutions in medium to large enterprises in a Business Systems Analyst role
Highly skilled in BI requirements gathering, business systems and data analysis; Experienced in Agile/Scrum delivery methodology writing user stories
Experience working with senior business leaders and other stakeholders in sales, finance, operations, and other functional areas to facilitate detailed requirements gathering and obtaining sign off
Exposure to enterprise reporting & data visualization tools such as Business Objects, Power BI, Tableau
Strong understanding of data warehouse and ETL processes with ability to write SQL queries to analyze data; Experience working with SAP BW, SAP ECC, ORACLE or other major ERP systems
Solid understanding of SAP functional modules such as Sales & Distribution, Order to Cash, Procure to Pay, Material Management, FICO etc. in a manufacturing industry
Ability to articulate business problems and solutions verbally and in writing (Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Visio etc.), research and collect data, establish facts, troubleshoot data issues
Ability to work on multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
Willingness to travel
