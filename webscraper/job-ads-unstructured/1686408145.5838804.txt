Machine Learning ENGINEER / Data SCIENTIST - to join our ML & Data team!

At Yoona.ai we're on a mission to build the only platform in the world based on Artificial Intelligence, hence Computer Machine Learning to help designers & decision makers to go get digital inspirations from data, transform from 2D to 3D, and from 3d to AR & VR.

Our engineering culture values pragmatism, honesty, and simplicity to solve hard problems the right way. We need you to design and build machine learning-powered products that help our customers learn from their data and make better decisions in real-time.

Your daily tasks will be drawing & applying machine learning algorithms and statistical techniques to build new product features together with an already existing product & tech team.


SKILLS REQUIRED:

4+ years of experience working in large scale data science environments
You have significant experience applying machine learning to real business problems
You have significant programming experience in Python
You have worked with Generative Adversarial Networks, Diffusion Models or Transformers in your past with a focus on Computer Vision
You are interested in Data Processing and Analysis Pipelines, in best case even have setup your own already
You know what Bias in Data Sets, how to detect and visualise it
(Bonus) You can translate a research paper into a PoC if the code does not exist

You must live in Berlin / Brandenburg and will have an hybrid work flow remote & workdays in our AI campus with the rest of the team.
