Your responsibilities

Co-ordinate team members to resolve data related problems that occur as well as implement solutions and preventive measures
Collaborate with the delivery teams to design and develop an end-to-end analytics solution
Work with Data SMEs and business to understand business requirements and design models accordingly
Design, create and test data models and data pipelines
Review data models and data pipelines designed by internal and external Data SMEs, architects and developers
Research new technologies, data modeling methods and information management systems and produce reports
Develop and set the data standards for Olympus' data architecture, ensuring that products under development are in line with chosen languages, data processes and principles
Participate in key decision-making bodies within the Insights & Analytics team to ensure alignment with the digital architecture
Functional leadership, motivation, development and assessment of team members so that their individual and collective performance is of the required standard and meets the current and future needs of the business

Your qualification

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mathematics, or other related field (or equivalent in working experience)
Long-standing experience as a solution architect in a data-related area and with data warehouse / data lakes solutions
Strong background in Star Schema or Data Vault based data modelling
Good knowledge of SQL and demonstrated ability to apply SQL creatively to a wide variety of business requirements
Experience in understanding and implementing data solutions using technologies such as Azure Data Factory, Matillion, Synapse Analytics and Power BI
Familiarity with data visualization tools such as Power BI and visualization standards (e.g. IBCS)
Good knowledge of data governance principles and system analysis
Basic knowledge and experience in project management, process modelling
Experienced and comfortable leading highly technical teams through agile development methodology
Several years of experience in managing staff, including work review, performance reviews and day-to-day coaching
Demonstrable interpersonal, problem-solving and coordinating skills
