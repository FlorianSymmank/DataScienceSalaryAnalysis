Conception, creation and further development of Power BI dashboards for different departments and applications
Collaboration with business units on data analysis, reporting and visualization
Strengthening and further development of a positive BI mindset in the organization through training and support of employees

Completed studies with an analytical background (e.g. business informatics), or computer science or IT systems, possibly also technician training with comparable qualifications and experience
Extensive knowledge in SAP, SAP Business Warehouse Power BI, SQL, Python, Visual Basic Microsoft Azure, ABB Zenon or comparable applications and database systems.
Experience in data analysis and visualization
A team player, independent and solution-oriented approach as well as very good written and verbal communication skills
