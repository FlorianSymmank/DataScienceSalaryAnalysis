We are looking for a german speaking Senior Data Analytics & Visualization Engineer to join our rapidly growing Data team in Germany to develop web applications for Visualisation of data and quantitative correlations that provide valuable insights to support data-driven decision-making. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and provide technical guidance to ensure the successful delivery of data-driven projects.

RESPONSIBILITIES
Develop web applications for the Visualisation of data and quantitative correlations
Develop and build sophisticated interactive dashboards e.g. with R and Shiny and/or alternative open source libraries such as Vue.js
Adaptation and optimisation of dashboards to Corporate Identity (CI) /Corporate Design (CD) based on templates
Coordination with clients to advise on new features and their design as well as to evaluate the feasibility and effort of their requests
Deployment and support in the operation of Dashboard including monitoring
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, assess data needs, and identify the most suitable analytics and visualization techniques for each project
Conduct in-depth data analysis to identify trends, patterns, and insights, and present findings to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner
Evaluate and recommend new data analytics and visualization tools, technologies, and methodologies to optimize data management and analytics capabilities
Provide technical guidance and support to the development and data teams throughout the project lifecycle
Collaborate with stakeholders to ensure data quality, security, and privacy in data analytics and visualization solutions
Assess the effectiveness of existing data analytics solutions and identify opportunities for improvement and optimisation

REQUIREMENTS
Bachelor's degree in a related field such as Computer Science, Mathematics, or Statistics or relevant work experience
Experience in data analytics and of developing web applications for Visualisation of data and quantitative correlation
Experience of successful project implementation in the design and creation of interactive dashboards using R/Shiny, Python and open-source libraries such as vue.js with customised corporate identity and corporate design
Experience in successfully operating data products (e.g. dashboards) - especially with regard to the issue of availability
Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and critical thinking skills
Proven ability to work independently as well as with wider EPAM and client technical/business teams
Strong interpersonal and communication skills
Fluent in German and English
