***This role is open to remote work from any location within Portugal or Spain***




Joining the Global HR Reporting and People Analytics Team as an Data Engineer you will be responsible for developing, implementing, and maintaining data pipelines and architectures that support the HR Function needs. You will be working closely with cross-functional teams, including business analysts, IT professionals, and HR experts, to design and implement data solutions to enable TUI to make informed business decisions.



ABOUT THE JOB




Developing and maintaining the data architecture in SAP BW4HANA

Managing the pipeline (collection, storage, access), engineering (cleansing, feature creation/selection), and understanding (documentation, exploration) of data

Assembling large, complex data sets that meet functional and technical business requirements

Building and maintaining architectures for optimal ETL from a wide variety of data sources using SAP BW4HANA, SQL big data technologies, flat files, and APIs

Ensure data quality and accuracy by performing data profiling, cleansing, and validation

Working with IT, data specialists, and other relevant team members to ensure that requested data and related process changes are scalable, secure, and compliant with company policies and regulations

Following and maintaining operational and control procedures as a part of an agile team

Collaborating with the business and HR experts to identify business requirements and translating them into data-driven solutions

Stay up to date with the latest trends and technologies in data engineering, and apply them to our business needs.




ABOUT YOU



Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field, highly advantageous
Strong professional experience delivering data solutions
Strong HANA (Cloud Environment) development background with experience in SAP BW4HANA
strong knowledge of data modeling, ETL processes, and data warehousing
Experience with relational SQL and NoSQL databases
Experience with Tableau data visualization tool
Experience with SAP SuccessFactors business content
Analytical, conceptual, and implementation skills
Technical skills in ABAP Core Data Services (CDS) modeling are a plus
Experience with data pipeline and workflow management tools such as Python or Airflow is a plus
Experience with AWS cloud services is a plus
