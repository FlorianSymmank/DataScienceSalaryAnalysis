Job Title: Product Manager - AI Integration - Medical Device Software Solutions

Join our growing team and be part of our mission to simplify the compliance process for medical device companies, helping them bring their next-generation technology to market faster.

As a Product Manager for AI Integration, you will be at the forefront of leveraging AI technologies to deliver value to our customers in the medical device industry. You will drive innovation and create seamless AI-integrated software solutions which will help guide them through the decision-making process in regulatory compliance.

Responsibilities:

Conduct in-depth research to identify and evaluate appropriate AI models for implementation within our products.
Design and build prototypes, collaborating closely with engineers to develop robust, optimised AI-integrated solutions.
Present final solutions to the team and successfully launch AI-integrated product modules.
Develop a deep understanding of regulatory impact in the medical device industry, familiarise
yourself with compliance regulations and stay updated on emerging trends.
Provide guidance and support to customers in effectively utilising AI capabilities to meet their
compliance needs and achieve desired outcomes, contributing to their success.

Requirements:

Bachelor's degree & Masters in computer science, data science, mathematics, statistics, or a related field with five to tens experience within a software company.
Passionate about AI, eager to stay ahead of the competition, and continuously learning about emerging trends and best practices in data science and the SaaS industry, particularly within the medical device compliance domain.
Possess a creative entrepreneurial mindset, demonstrating curiosity, quick thinking, and the ability to work under project deadlines without compromising quality.
Proficient in programming applications and data processing code
Familiarity with data pre-processing, feature engineering, and data visualization techniques.
Experience working with large datasets and querying languages (e.g., SQL) for data extraction and manipulation.
Exceptional analytical and problem-solving skills, enabling you to derive actionable insights from complex compliance data.
