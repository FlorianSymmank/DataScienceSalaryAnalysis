Your role

In cross-functional teams, you will drive & manage projects in the field of Machine Learning (ML) and data science. In this role, you will support the planning and execution of multiple Data Science/ ML projects. As part of a vivid team, you will build strategies for assessing and planning data science and machine learning-related tasks like gathering and assessing requirements, and reporting outcomes and results. You will help collect learnings to be integrated into a new project. With your organizational and project management skills, you will enable our experts to focus on technical, high-quality solutions. You will be at the interface between our technical experts and ZEISS departments. You will be an advocate of the work of our data and analytics team.
Your profile
An excellent university degree (Ph.D. is a plus) in natural science, mathematics, computer science, engineering, or similar
At least 5+ years of professional experience in a leading project management function, ideally in machine learning / Data Science
At least 2+ years of team leadership (disciplinary or matrix organization)
The ability to understand the technical architecture of ML/ Data Science solutions
Experiences with best practices in model development, deployment, and operation
A good understanding of the power of standardization at the core and innovative flexibility at the shell
Experienced in stakeholder management
The ability to understand customer needs and solve real-world problems
Strong communication, presentation, and project management skills
Fluent English and advanced German language skills
