We are looking for a highly skilled, German speaking Senior Data Scientist (Data Analytics Modeling) to join our rapidly growing Data team in Germany. You will be responsible for development of predictive analytics models and implementation of real-time applications, maintaining data-driven solutions that provide valuable insights and drive business growth for a wide range of client problems. You will be responsible for designing, implementing, and You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and provide technical guidance to ensure the successful delivery of data-driven projects.

RESPONSIBILITIES
Development of predictive analytics models, design, implement, and maintain data science solutions, including machine learning models, predictive analytics, and statistical analyses for multiple client requirements
Design and implementation of complex real-time applications based on data streams (e.g. Kafka)
Comprehensive support in the conception (especially. method selection), implementation and optimisation of predictive models
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, assess data needs, and identify the most suitable data science techniques for each project
Develop and maintain high-quality technical documentation, including model documentation, validation reports, and user guides
Conduct in-depth data analysis to identify trends, patterns, and insights, and present findings to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner
Evaluate and recommend new data science tools, technologies, and methodologies to optimize data management and analytics capabilities
Provide technical guidance and support to the development and data teams throughout the project lifecycle
Collaborate with EPAM and client teams to ensure data quality, security, and privacy in data science solutions
Assess the effectiveness of existing data science solutions and identify opportunities for improvement and optimisation

REQUIREMENTS
Bachelor's degree in a related field such as Computer Science, Mathematics, or Statistics or relevant work experience
Extensive experience as a Senior Data Scientist involved in Data Analytics Modeling
Strong demonstrable expertise in the conception (incl. method selection) and development of predictive analytics models for a wide range of issues - preferably for economic and scientific issues
Strong demonstrable expertise in the conception and implementation of real-time applications based on data streams (e.g. Kafka)
Evidence of cooperation with research and science for participation in publications, preparation of scientific reports, etc
Familiarity with cloud-based data platforms and services, such as AWS, Azure, or GCP, is a plus
Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and critical thinking skills
