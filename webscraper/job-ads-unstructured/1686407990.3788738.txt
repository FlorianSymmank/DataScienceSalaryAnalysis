Job Description

Position: ML/MLOps Engineer

Location: Stuttgart Region

Salary Range: Up to 95k (depending on experience)

Xcede has partnered with a sensors manufacturing company who are looking to further grow their data science and machine learning team. They are looking for a skilled candidate to work as a ML/MLOps Engineer and support data scientists with the software development and deployment of the ML models.

Job Responsibilities:

Collaborate with a diverse team of experts to develop and enhance the machine learning products
Work alongside data scientists to create and implement software and architecture for the ML applications
Review code and improve the codebase
Optimise architectures and algorithms for use in the edge applications, like real-time monitoring of critical infrastructure
Help shape the future of distributed fiber optic sensor technology in a wide variety of new applications

Requirements:

Hold a technical degree in computer science, physics, mathematics, or engineering
Have some practical experience in software or machine learning environments
Be proficient in Python programming, and familiarity with embedded languages is beneficial
Be familiar with software development and testing best practices
Possess excellent communication and presentation skills in German and English
Have knowledge of additional technologies like Python ML frameworks (e.g., PyTorch), GitLab, virtualization and containerization, TensorRT, CUDA, model and data versioning, and frontend development as an added advantage

The position can be English-speaking, but writing and speaking in German is a big plus.

Please apply with your updated resume.
