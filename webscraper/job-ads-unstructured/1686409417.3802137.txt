Senior Data Scientist (f/m/d)
Bad Vilbel | Deutschland (DE) | Full-time | Permanent


Our position is located in the IT department of the STADA Group and will have a direct line reporting to the Director Data Science & Process Mining. If you are a self-motivated individual with a passion for data science and with a track record of delivering impactful insights, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity. We are seeking a skilled data scientist with 4-6+ years of work experience to join our Data Science Team. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in statistics, mathematics, and computer science, and will be able to analyze and interpret complex data sets to identify patterns and insights. As a data scientist, you will consult the business, identify new data science use-cases and drive change management by working closely with cross-functional teams to develop solutions that drive business outcomes and improve decision-making processes.


What you can expect:
You lead and run Data Science projects across all business areas
You analyze and interpret complex data sets using statistical methods and machine learning techniques
You are the platform lead for our Machine Learning infrastructure
You communicate complex analytical findings to stakeholders and provide recommendations based on insights
You develop predictive models and algorithms to identify patterns and trends in data
You work collaboratively with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and develop data-driven solutions
You build and maintain data pipelines and infrastructure to support ongoing data analysis


Who we are looking for:
You hold a Bachelor's, master's or higher degree in computer science, statistics, mathematics, or a related field
You have 4-6+ years of work experience in data science, analytics, or a related field
You bring strong programming skills in at least one language like Python, R, JAVA and/or SQL
You have experience in visual Data Transformation tools like Alteryx or Azure Data Factory is a plus
You are experienced with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI
Previous work experience using Databricks is a plus
You have strong analytical and problem-solving skills
You have strong communication and collaboration skills


What we offer:

Job ticket for the RMV region and Job Bike

Numerous additional benefits such as "future payment", group accident insurance, supplementary pension scheme, and chemical industry pension fund
