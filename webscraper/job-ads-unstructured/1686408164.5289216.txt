Job brief

As a Data Scientist at aimpower you will use your Python programming skills to build and maintain machine learning and deep learning pipelines for predicting communication effectiveness in the marketing context. This will involve working closely with cross-functional teams to understand the needs of our clients and internal teams and using data analysis and machine learning techniques to design and implement solutions that meet those needs. You will be responsible for collecting and pre-processing data from multiple sources, building predictive models, and implementing data science pipelines to drive tangible business results.

In this role you will:

Use Python to build NLP/CV applications, develop and train ML models and ensure they are effective
Identify and generate relevant datasets, and implement appropriate analysis methodologies while maintaining high standards of rigor and clear documentation to enable replication.
Resolve complex optimization problems resulting in tangible benefits.
Deliver business outcomes from the application of machine learning and advanced analytics.
Identify process improvements and develop automated solutions
Build relationships with stakeholders across our company (R&D, Tech, Customer Success and Sales) to ensure we are using data science in the core of our products
Use data analysis techniques to extract insights from data, being able to communicate your findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.
Build your knowledge and learn new skills & share them with the rest of the team
Keep up-to-date with the latest data science and machine learning techniques and technologies

About us

We are aimpower, a fast-growing marketing technology startup founded in 2020. We enable marketers to test any type of advertising in minutes for what really matters: Creative effectiveness from the consumer's point of view. Be it a TV commercial, social media content, packaging design, print ad, e-commerce content or PoS material. All delivered through easy-to-use tools on our brainsuite SaaS platform - a digital consumer brain, based on neuroscience and powered by the latest AI technology. Our mission is to empower marketing teams around the world to launch highly effective communications, to make faster, evidence-based creative decisions, and, most importantly, spend their time and budgets better than on ineffective advertising. Our clients include companies in 25 countries worldwide.
