Your responsibilities

Responsibility for data privacy & protection related support for subsidiaries and the Headquarter in close collaboration with the Group IT
Crucial role in shaping the future of the company’s data policies & data compliance within the data governance organization and actively contribute to the success of the digital strategy at PERI
Participation in innovative projects on developing new services and digital products

Project management experience and the ability to drive topics

Expertise in defining, documenting and implementing data governance best practices, policies & procedures in a large organization
