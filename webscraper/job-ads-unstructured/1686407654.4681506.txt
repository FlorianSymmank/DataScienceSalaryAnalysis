ABOUT THE NORDEX GROUP

The manufacture of wind energy plants in the on-shore segment has been our core competence and passion for around 35 years. With more than 43 GW installed capacity worldwide, our turbines supply sustainable energy to more than 80% of the global energy market and we are one of the largest companies in the wind industry. National subsidiaries in more than 30 markets and production facilities in Germany, Spain, Brazil, the USA, Mexico and India offer our more than 9,000 employees the opportunity for international and intercultural cooperation.





We are growing and together driving forward the expansion of alternative energies worldwide - if you wish, from now on with you. Join the #TeamNordex

Head of Data Analysis DEU (m/f/d)
REQUISITION ID: 2902
LOCATION: Hamburg, DE, 22419
DEPARTMENT: Service

YOUR TASKS

You hire, scale and nurture a team of senior Data Scientists. You keep them motivated by achieving an optimal balance between achievement and challenge
The team is the central contact point for analytics demand in DEU region
You work closely with the Engineering & ITO to enable Data Scientists to obtain the data they need, by building their own pipelines while guaranteeing an optimal and organic evolution of our ETL/ELT processes
As product owner you and your team take the responsibility to deliver cross-functional analytics projects from end-to-end (from ideation to roll out)
You define and execute the analytics strategy
You take an active part in community management, training and communication
You drive data literacy and data culture across Nordex Service
You scan the market for developments in advanced analytics



YOUR PROFILE

5+ years of experience managing BI and advance analytics teams
Experience of designing and deploying machine learning algorithms at scale
University degree in Business, Economics or Information Technology
You are an expert in working hands-on with data coming from a diverse variety of sources: knowledge of R, Shiny, SQL, familiarity with PYTHON
Experience in data warehousing and business intelligence reporting using Power BI, Tableau, or Tibco Spotfire
Knowledge in cloud-based Machine Learning and Data Science products
Solid experience in data analysis and reporting
Knowledge of data mining and segmentation techniques
You have very good communication and leadership skills as well as intercultural awareness
Enjoy new challenges in a motivated and dynamic environment
Hands-on mentality and decision-making personality
Good written and verbal communication skills in English
