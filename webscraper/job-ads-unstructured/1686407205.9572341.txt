What to Expect

Tesla is accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy.This is only possible through extraordinary speed, innovation and efficiency. Gigafactory Berlin forms the perfect basis for rolling out Tesla's incredible success story in Europe. The most important pillar for this are our employees. Their passion, motivation and engagement ensure that we achieve our goals. We are looking for you to continue and expand this success story together.


What You’ll Do
Identifying trends and generate insights by conducting analyses of Tesla´s data systems
Convey crucial findings with internal stakeholders to encourage data-driven storytelling and decision-making
Identify critical metrics and develop high-level dashboards to monitor the advancement of the company's KPIs and its top objectives
Constantly pursue methods to increase operational effectiveness
Engage in internal stakeholder meetings and present status updates on a weekly basis
Create reports and keep track of project performance using spreadsheets and data metrics
Support the Program Manager to collect data from cross functional teams
What You’ll Bring
Studying towards a degree in Data Science, Supply Chain Management, Purchasing or Economics
Experience with data analytics programs like Tableau, PowerBI, etc.
Excellent analytical skills including advanced Excel (ie. macros, VBA)
Preferable previous experience in Supply chain processes and procurement
Critical understanding for statistics and data
Strong presentation and communication skills
