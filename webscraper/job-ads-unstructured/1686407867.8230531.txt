CONXAI is building an AI platform for the AEC industry to quickly and easily contextualize different types & formats of project lifecycle data from multiple sources, and transform them into actionable knowledge.

Since its founding in 2021 in Germany, CONXAI has focussed on effortlessly equipping AEC teams with the data and knowledge they need to deliver higher performance through automated decision support, planning and workflows.

CONXAI is building its core technology that can tackle multiple AEC-focussed use cases with multiple different data types from a wide variety of sources.

Tasks

We are looking for full-time interns to solve some cutting-edge data science problems and be a part of our product development. You should have experience in information extraction with Python, data analysis, visualization, analytics, and SQL. The successful candidate will work on data extraction, processing, analysis, and visualization to support decision-making processes.


Extract, clean, and pre-process data from different sources using Python
Develop and implement data analysis techniques to extract insights from data
Create visualizations to communicate complex data insights to non-technical stakeholders
Support the development of predictive models using machine learning techniques
Perform ad-hoc data analysis and reporting as required
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support data-driven decision-making processes
Requirements


Pursuing a Master's degree in Data Science or related field
Experience in information extraction with Python
Proficient in data analysis, visualization, and analytics
Strong knowledge of SQL
Good communication and interpersonal skills
Ability to work in a team-oriented environment
Strong problem-solving skills
Fluent English skills
This is a unique opportunity to apply your academic knowledge & skills in real world applications and learn from experts.
