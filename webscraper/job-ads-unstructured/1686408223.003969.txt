Project Description

In this project, Luxoft helps the customer, which is a German manufacturer, to develop, train, and deploy Machine Learning models for internal customer needs.

Some of the models include:

Automatic Warranty Claim Approval
Financial Forecasting
Automatic anomaly detection in engine signals (IoT on mining trucks)

The customer develops engines for mining trucks, yachts, cruise ships, power generators, etc.
