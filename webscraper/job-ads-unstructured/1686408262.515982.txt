Research Assistant (f/m/d) – Neuroinflammation
Full-time, permanent
At Evotec’s headquarters in Hamburg, Germany, the Neuroinflammation & Data Science group is seeking a highly motivated and dedicated
Research Assistant (f/m/d) – Neuroinflammation
Your key responsibilities:
Perform cellular, molecular biology and biochemical laboratory experiments
Contribute to the development of biochemical and/or immuno-assays using primary cells, cell lines or iPSC-derived glial cells and/or neurons for identification of novel compounds treating neuroinflammatory diseases
Support development of translational assays for driving small molecule development towards the clinic
Plan experiments and analysis of the results for discussion within project team
Ensure quality and timely delivery of data
Record experiments punctually in electronic notebook
Your qualifications:
Successfully completed apprenticeship (BTA/CTA/MTA) with some years of hands-on laboratory experience or BSc. Or MSc. in the Life Sciences
Hands-on experience in cell culture and image or immunoassay technologies (e.g. Immunocytochemistry, ELISA, MSD, Singulex, HTRF, Western blotting)
Experience with standard molecular and biochemical techniques such as transient transfections, qPCR, etc. is a plus
Dynamic personality with high levels of drive, enthusiasm and commitment and ability to cope with pressure
Excellent team spirit and capability of working with a wide degree of autonomy and independence
Ability to work in a cross-functional team
Fluency in written and spoken English
Our offer:
A permanent position within a vigorous and exciting professional environment promoted by an open culture and a spirit of community
A diverse, international workforce with a dynamic working environment that fosters creativity, innovations and teamwork
30 days of annual holiday, monthly allowance for public transportation, and in-house canteen
Capital forming benefits, flexible working hours, holiday pay, and annual bonus depending on performance
