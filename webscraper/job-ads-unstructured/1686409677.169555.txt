YOUR TASKS

Smart Steel Technologies is a fast-growing Berlin-based startup, revolutionizing steel production by reducing energy and carbon footprint through machine learning. We are seeking an experienced Infrastructure Engineer to join our team and develop scalable, reliable, and efficient infrastructure for our machine learning solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and maintain event-driven architecture and data pipelines for machine learning applications in an industrial production environment

Work with DevOps, software, and ML engineers to integrate ML models into production systems, ensuring scalability, reliability, and performance

Build monitoring tools and anomaly detection systems to ensure data integrity and auditability

Research and implement new technologies to enhance ML infrastructure and workflows

Participate in cross-stakeholder collaboration and decision-making processes

At least 3 years of experience in designing, developing, and maintaining ML infrastructure and workflows

Excellent communication and problem-solving skills, with the ability to work in a team environment

Proficiency in event-driven architectures, event sourcing with Kafka (preferred) and/or other message passing technologies (e.g., RabbitMQ, IBM MQ)

Experience with stream processing within the Kafka ecosystem (KafkaConnect, KafkaStreams, ksqlDB, Confluent Schema Registry, etc.) is an advantage

Strong programming skills in Python (preferred) and/or one or more other modern languages, such as Java, Scala, Modern C++, C#

Solid understanding of distributed systems, cloud computing, and containerization technologies, including Kubernetes and Docker

Familiarity with software engineering best practices and Agile development methodologies

Familiarity with relational (e.g., PostgreSQL, Oracle) and time-series databases (e.g., ClickHouse, InfluxDB)

Prior experience in industrial settings (e.g., SCADA, industrial IoT) is an advantage

About us

Smart Steel Technologies supplies ready-to-use artificial intelligence software products for process optimization in steel mills that lead to improved quality, reduction of energy cost and improvement of CO2 efficiency. The machine learning and data science we employ comes in several varieties: image processing, signal processing, classical supervised modeling, unsupervised methods, and fundamental statistics and probability. We are hiring a talented Engineers and Developer to join our team and help us to further develop our industry leading solutions.
