Do you want to learn about our processes during your training and actively support the digitalization of our data? Then become a part of TecAlliance!

Job Definition

What you will learn in your apprenticeship:

Knowing and using programming languages
Analysing processes, identifying optimisation opportunities and uncover and eliminate weaknesses, as well as digitise them
Solving tricky problems
Testing applications before the customer can find the bugs
Data protection is not just a word


Job Qualification

What you should bring with you:

A good secondary school leaving certificate or better A levels (or other university entrance qualification)
Interest in mathematics and computer science
German language skills at level C1
Good language skills in English
Willingness to work at other locations


University drop-outs are also welcome! With a certain previous education there is the possibility of shortening the training.
