For our locations in Germany we are looking for several Data Analysts (m/f/d) as soon as possible.


As a Data Analyst (m/f/d) with Power BI skills, you will be responsible for analyzing and interpreting large volumes of data to gain valuable insights and findings. You will collaborate closely with various teams to make data-driven decisions and improve business outcomes. Your main task will involve extracting, transforming, and visualizing complex data to create meaningful reports and dashboards.

What you will move with us
Data extraction and transformation: You will gather data from different sources, clean it, and transform it into the required format for analysis.
Data analysis and interpretation: You will utilize statistical methods and data analysis techniques to identify patterns, trends, and relationships within the data. You will develop statistical models and hypotheses to gain informed insights.
Power BI development: You will use Power BI to create data visualizations, interactive dashboards, and reports. You will design engaging and user-friendly visualizations to present complex data in an understandable manner.
Data validation and verification: You will ensure the accuracy and reliability of the data used. You will perform data quality checks and verify data integrity to ensure precise analysis results.
Collaboration with stakeholders: You will closely collaborate with internal teams to understand their requirements and provide them with data-driven insights and solutions. You will effectively communicate with technical and non-technical stakeholders to present results and provide recommendations.

Extensive knowledge and experience in using Power BI
