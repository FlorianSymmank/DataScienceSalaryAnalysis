Summary:
For our German site in Eningen unter Achalm, we are looking for a talented Software Engineer (m/f/d) who will help our sophisticated team of engineers deliver VIAVI’s new cutting edge telecom field test instruments. You will work on implementing software for different microcontroller- and microprocessor-based products within an established and collaborative team. This position is for you, if you are curious about new technology, embrace knowledge diversity, and if you are ambitious about your work results.
Duties & Responsibilities:
Development of reliable, cost-effective and high-quality solutions
Working on prototyping, definition and analysis of requirements, high- and low-level design, as well as testing and verification
Programming of hardware-related firmware for embedded systems
Programming of application software and/or GUI software
Working on projects within international teams (USA/France)
Maintaining and enhancing existing code bases
Pre-Requisites / Skills / Experience Requirements:
Bachelor’s degree in (technical) computer science, electrical engineering, or related
Basic technical knowledge of embedded software systems
Experience or understanding of firmware design tools and languages
Strong programming skills on C, C++, and Python
Skills in Qt/QML are beneficial
Strong initiative and eagerness to learn
Good teamwork skills
Good analytical and problem-solving skills
Good written and verbal communication skills in German (B2 level or higher) and English
Basic knowledge of telecommunication engineering and experience with Linux would be beneficial but is not essential
Benefits:

Attractive remuneration and profit-sharing
About VIAVI Solutions:
VIAVI Solutions (NASDAQ: VIAV) has a 100-year history of technical innovations that have evolved to keep pace and address our customer’s most pressing business issues. We make equipment, software, and systems that help to plan, deploy, certify, monitor, and optimize all kinds of networks - like those for mobile phones, service providers, large businesses, and data centers.
VIAVI employees are fierce about supporting customer success and we welcome people who bring their best every day to the company - to question, to collaborate and to push for solutions that will delight our customers.
