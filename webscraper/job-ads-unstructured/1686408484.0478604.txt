Purpose of Position

Data sits at the heart of the company. This role is to ensure that Awin is able to leverage the data it has access to across the group and build out reporting to ensure we are able to make better commercial decisions and provide best-in-class campaign management to support client facing departments.

As an AI/ML Engineer, your responsibility is to research, build and design and automate predictive models along with metrics to track there progress. You have proven communication and analysis skills. You are a strong team player, able to work with minimal supervision and keen to implement and improve our standards and working practices.

You understand data and business requirements and are able to translate into technical requirements. You write clean and high-quality code, easy to understand and maintain. You are able to implement various methodologies of data extraction, transformation and storage and successfully apply your experience and knowledge to create new and improve existing predictive models.

Within the role, you are able to break down requirements with clear instructions. As a member of Data Services department you will be working with a global team of data engineers and data analysts amongst other big data engineers within the organization.
