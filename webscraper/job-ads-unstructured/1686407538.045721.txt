Are you a German speaking senior technologist in the field of data & analytics and data science? Are you hungry to thrive in an entrepreneurial environment and take ownership for critical technical and business decisions that directly impact EPAM and our clients? If so, then EPAM has a great opportunity for you to join us as a Manager/Senior Manager - Data Analytics & Data Science Strategy Consultant (m/f/d) in Germany where you will provide expertise in strategic, conceptual consulting for clients in Data Analytics and Data Science projects.
This is a highly visible role within our hugely expanding Data & Analytics Practice where you will have strong autonomy to drive the relevant outcomes for the clients.

RESPONSIBILITIES
Lead on data analytics consulting projects and strategies for clients, including identifying business needs, defining goals, and developing roadmaps for implementation of projects in relation to the organisational classification of Data Science
Play a key role in identifying suitable technologies for the implementation of Data Science programs
Work closely with teams of data analysts, data scientists, architects and delivery managers to drive high-quality services and solutions to clients
Develop and maintain relationships with clients, wider EPAM teams, and participate in business development activities to help win new business
Stay up to date on emerging data analytics and data science trends and technologies, and continuously building consultative skills and industry knowledge within the team

REQUIREMENTS
Bachelor's degree in a related field such as Computer Science, Mathematics, or Statistics or relevant work experience
Extensive experience in strategic, conceptual consulting for clients in the field of data analytics and data science
A proven track record of working with technical teams, scoping engagements, and delivering results for clients
Experience of acting in an advisory capacity with clients to move their requirements and vision to a technology and strategic direction for the implementation of relevant solutions
Able to articulate the pros and cons of technology choices and solution design decisions and to construct and present rational options to clients and prospective clients on the options they have
Confident in expressing points-of-view, making recommendations and presenting analysis and recommendations up to board level where appropriate
Ability to operate at the strategic level, yet being close enough to the details to add value to clients and be a real support to your team
Strong interpersonal and communication skills
Fluent German and English speaker
