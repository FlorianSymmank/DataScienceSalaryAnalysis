The Computer Vision & Graphics group of the Vision & Imaging Technologies (VIT) department is currently offering a master's thesis in the area of "Robust long-term registration of LIDAR and RGB data". Become a part of our team and join us on our journey of research and innovation!




What you will do

The starting point of the work will be a detailed research on the state of the art in this area, which will illuminate different approaches

Based on this, identified, suitable methods are to be implemented and, if necessary, further developed

The final part of the work will be the evaluation on synthetic and real data sets




What you bring to the table

Master's student in the field of computer science, mathematics or an equivalent subject at a Berlin or Brandenburg university with above-average performance

Profound knowledge in computer vision

Independent and structured way of working




What you can expect

Fascinating challenges in a scientific and entrepreneurial setting

Modern and excellently equipped workspace in central location

Great and cooperative working atmosphere in an international team
