Your responsibilities

Work with the Data Lead (Domain) to drive the end to end process for managing master data
Contribute to the development of actionable reporting that monitors and pinpoints data requirements and challenges
Support in the analysis and enrichment of master data elements and optimizing the flow of data between users and stakeholders
Examine the data management-related activities to propose and plan initiatives that optimize processes and capabilities
Work closely with the business, providing advice in how it can leverage master data to support strategic goals
Design and implement solutions to ensure data consolidation and cleansing from across the organization into a single, consistent data storage platform.
Research the latest Data Management best practices, principles and processes and monitor adherence, proposing any remediation actions needed
Create and deliver the product data curation processes including negotiation and change management of data creation with appropriate meta data and embedding of new processes to standardize product data
