Position:
Graduate Data Analyst

Job Description:

Join Our Arrow's Graduate Program Designed for the Next Generation of Innovators

Our Arrow's EMEA Graduate Program is a 12-month journey designed specifically for graduates who completed their education not more than 2 years ago and are seeking to take part in a practical learning curriculum. We provide a platform to work with experienced mentors to accelerate your career and establish a solid foundation to build and grow your engineering career at Arrow. During the program, selected candidates will improve both professional and soft skills and will be able to meet with and learn from experts across the business. By the end of the program, you will have the ground to further develop a successful career in Arrow.

Visit our Graduate Program website for more information.

What you should expect from the role:

Arrow believes that the best way for you to grow is by giving you full responsibility for your tasks. As part of your personal and group projects, you'll have the opportunity to engage in practical work and will work alongside highly specialized experts on the following:

Supporting domain experts to build and validate conceptual models.
Developing & providing BI solutions for the various EMEA business functions.
Support in estimator selection as well as building, training, and evaluating models which will provide the foundation of automating various business processes across EMEA.
Performing data preparation: data cleaning, outlier detection, handling missing data etc. in order to get ready for building fast and accurate Machine Learning models & meaningful data analytics.
Other, specific to the Graduate program: Take responsibility for projects (individual and in group) and create innovative solutions that have an impact on what Arrow does as a business.

Requirements:

You must have a legal right to work in the country where the position is located.
You should have successfully completed your computer science, mathematics, statistics or similar quantitative field degree or other relevant degree (B.Sc., M.Sc. allowing you to have a full understanding of relational databases and ability to extract and query data independently with SQL) before October 2023 or not more than 2 years ago.
You should be familiar with all or some of the following:
proficiency with visualization tools (e.g., Power BI, tableau, Qlik)
object oriented programming, knowledge of Python and writing scalable code
Data Base experience (MySQL or similar)
Expert understanding of MS Office tools, primarily Excel
visualization of insights with Python (matplotlib, seaborn etc.)
Advantageous but not essential to have experience in the following:
good understanding of Data Science and Machine Learning methods (clustering, regression, decision trees and random forests etc.)
experience with jupyter notebooks and sklearn (libraries numpy, pandas, SciPy etc.)
experience with cloud-based platforms (Azure, AWS)
experience with statistical modelling with R
Business fluent English & German (oral & written) are key requirements for this position.
You must be flexible and have geographical mobility.
Creative, outside-the-box thinker who brings something new to the table and is not afraid to share those ideas.

What's in it for you?

A competitive salary, holiday entitlement and company benefits according to local standards
A permanent working contract
A mix of professional and soft skills trainings
Group projects to work on business solutions with other program members from multiple countries, cultures, and functions
Networking in the organization
Support and career guidance from a mentor
Wide range of career development opportunities
Access to LinkedIn Learning

How to apply

To apply, please click on "Apply" below and submit your CV with a letter of motivation ( both in English) providing responses to the following questions:

What was your decision-making process in selecting your current course/degree discipline?
